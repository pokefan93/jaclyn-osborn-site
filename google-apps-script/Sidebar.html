Use this panel instead of digging through the technical tabs. The editing view hides the Stripe housekeeping and keeps the sheet focused on what Jaclyn actually needs to change.

Editing view

Shows the main tabs, adds checkboxes and dropdowns, and hides the Stripe housekeeping columns.

Quick jumps
How to update

Book details: use the Books tab.

Sold out / preorder / signed copy notes: use the Availability & Notes tab.

Direct sale price: use the Direct Sales tab. Type the price in cents, so 1999 means $19.99.

Retailer links: use the Store Links tab.

Publish

When you click publish, the sheet is sent to GitHub. GitHub then updates Stripe, rebuilds the site, and deploys it.
